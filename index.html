About SEED

The De-notified, Nomadic and Semi-Nomadic Tribes are the most neglected, marginalized and economically and socially deprived communities. Most of them have been living a life of destitution for generations; and still continue to do so with an uncertain and gloomy future. De-notified, Nomadic and Semi-nomadic Tribes somehow escaped the attention of our development framework and thus are deprived of the support unlike Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes.
